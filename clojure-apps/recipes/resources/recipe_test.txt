  <p class="P69"><a id="_GoBack"></a><span class="T1">Skillet Chicken Macaroni</span></p>
  <p class="P3">&nbsp;</p>
  <p class="P9"><span class="T6">1 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; stick butter&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; ⅔ lg&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; chopped onion</span></p>
  <p class="P9"><span class="T6">6 c&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; elbow macaroni&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; 2 c &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; diced, cooked chicken</span></p>
  <p class="P9"><span class="T6">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; grated cheddar cheese&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; 3 15-oz.&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; cans tomato sauce</span></p>
  <p class="P9"><span class="T6">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; 3 cans&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; water</span> <span class="T8">- or more</span><span class="T6">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; salt &amp; pepper</span></p>
  <p class="P3">&nbsp;</p>
  <p class="P10"><span class="T6">Sauté onion in butter in very large skillet. &nbsp;Mix dry macaroni in butter and onion till coated and yellowed. &nbsp;Add tomato sauce and water and seasonings. &nbsp;Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. &nbsp;Add water if needed.</span></p>
  <p class="P3">&nbsp;</p>
  <p class="P10"><span class="T6">Add chicken that has been pulled apart. &nbsp;Cook 5 minutes more. &nbsp;Cover with grated cheese and simmer until cheese melts.</span></p>
  <p class="P3">&nbsp;</p>
  <p class="P10"><span class="T6">Serves 14-16. &nbsp;This is tripled from the original recipe.</span></p>
